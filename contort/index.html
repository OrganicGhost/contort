Contortium Theatre: Rude, Gruesome, and Scary!

Channelling the spirit of the theatre of horror and comedy, the twisted and the bizarre, and the legendary Theatre of the Grand Guignol.

A new writing theatre company based in West Yorkshire in the UK

Specialising in the short, the shocking, and the very, very, strange. A very different night out. Not Safe For... anyone really.

A Note From the Director:

Expect an evening of modern Grand Guignol, alternating horror and comedy. Presented for a general audience.

Audiences will witness to a set of six to eight short pieces, depending on desired runtime. Each with a running time of approximately five to fifteen minutes, including 15 minute interval.

Involves horror and stage blood strictly adult, to young adult only. Not suitable for children.
